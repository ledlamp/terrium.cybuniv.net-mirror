Title: The Terrium Buggy
Post by: GTR34 on June 15, 2013, 11:53:47 PM
Hello all, GTR34 here!

I have modeled the first of the 4 mars vehicles, the buggy. There are some substantial model improvements over the last including:

  • Detailed suspension with working components
    Higher quality mesh
    Texture mapping throughout the vehicle
    Alpha maps to dictate the shininess of exposed surfaces or surfaces that are close to each other
    Ambient occlusion to add shadow depth
    Shadow map to add further shadow depth
    Tail lights and light flares for each LED bulb

For the first time, I have not rendered this in modo. This buggy is 100% real time rendered in the Unity game engine. Take a look!
