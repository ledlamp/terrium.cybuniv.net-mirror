Title: An updated To-Do list, and general information about development
Post by: Ace on August 10, 2013, 05:16:39 AM
Hello!


I am aware that no-one has had any updates from the development team since Eric and I stopped updating the forums on Mars. Well... here's some information for you.


Firstly, the reason we've had very slow progress: vacations. Eric's been away for a few weeks, and has hardly been around over the past month. GTR vanished part-way through making the new tank, in order to "set up for college" if I remember correctly - which is a fair excuse. So that's left me alone to work on the project, and I'll be honest, I've taken a few breaks of one or two days every now and again.


The current status: Well... basic networking works. Positions sync across all computers in a server, and lasers fire when (and sometimes where) they're supposed to. The physics of the buggy has been revamped a few times, to make it more realistic and to improve high-speed handling.


And the future: Rather than type out a lengthy statement, I'm just going to paste our current ToDo list. It's a very short-term thing. I personally hope to have all the things mentioned on it checked off by the end of the month.



Networking:
1. Reported not working. Unlikely to be the case but I want to check over it, just in case.
2. Lasers - collision and sync is not working
3. Glitchy collision physics? Needs looking into, again.


3D Modeling:
1. Buggy needs a floor √ (Done)
2. Tank needs finishing
3. Jet needs starting


Graphics:
1. Underwater effects needed


Music:
1. Finish Menu Piece
2. Start Theme Piece




Finally, I'm quitting development for a few months, starting from September, for exams. I'll be back after the exam period, which means I'll be working again in December, hopefully.


In the meantime, we continue to update the Alpha build (available on the cybuniv site, just follow the signs). :)
