Re: Terrium Buggy Custom Models?

In a later Terrium version (1.1+), we will implement a feature that allows you to select from various vehicle designs for the buggy, hovercraft, jet, and tank. For example, we may include GTR's retro design, the classic buggy, and the Terrium buggy for the various buggy models. Then you choose which one you want to be default, so when you join a game, that's the one everyone sees you using.

You will not be able to upload your own models to the game, however. You'll have to choose from the in-game selection.
