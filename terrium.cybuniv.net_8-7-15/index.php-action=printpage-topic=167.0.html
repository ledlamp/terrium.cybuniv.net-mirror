Title: Terrium Game and Forum Update: July 29, 2015
Post by: Eric on July 29, 2015, 06:22:03 PM
Greetings!


The status of the Terrium rewrite is good! First, I have some news regarding Cybuniv, this forum, and the game.

Cybuniv (http://cybuniv.net/), my old game portal project, serves little purpose at the moment, as Terrium is its only game. Considering that Terrium takes precedence over any of my projects at the moment, it would be inappropriate for me to keep the forum subsidiary to Cybuniv. Therefore, I have decided to shut down Cybuniv and give Terrium its own domain name. I will also be switching Terrium's forum software from SMF to XenForo. The site design will be totally revamped with a slick and modern look . . . and yes, it will include a new AWESOME background planetary space design. In a few days, we should be good to go on terrium.net (http://terrium.net/)!

With regard to the Terrium rewrite, my role has evolved to that of project manager. I have hired freelance Unity developer Tomislav Bali (http://tomislavba.li/) as lead programmer. The game is being programmed in Unity 5 Pro, using C#, Unity's new networking (UNET), and Unity's new GUI (UGUI). Quite an upgrade!

We already completed two milestones: the lobby and world selector/loader. The game, player, and server settings are almost complete. Vehicle physics are on their way too. We also set up a new in-game world editor for Terrium Beta 1.0—no more trying to find someone with Unity 3 Pro to export your "Whirlds (http://wiki.unity3d.com/index.php?title=Whirld)" like in Mars Explorer (http://marsxplr.com)! Next update, when the vehicles are good to go, I will share an alpha build for you all to test.

Check out the following Google doc for more details on Terrium's progress: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1JZ8AbCx5Fr_4gGykNbx7T4a7MNJfbhzRFDaciQbdgzs/edit?usp=sharing (https://docs.google.com/document/d/1JZ8AbCx5Fr_4gGykNbx7T4a7MNJfbhzRFDaciQbdgzs/edit?usp=sharing)

That is all I want to comment on for now. Stay tuned for more big updates over the next couple weeks!


Eric
