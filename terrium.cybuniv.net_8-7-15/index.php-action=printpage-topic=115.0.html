What about a Steering Wheel/Pilot Wheel for the Terrium Vehicles, let me explain.

Buggys, tanks, jets and hovercrafts should have a Steering Wheel/Pilot Wheel in them (tanks shouldn't have pilot wheels; jets and hovercrafts should have ONLY pilot wheels.)

Steering Wheel: Used to Steer the vehicle while on the ground by tilting left/right.

Pilot Wheel: Used to tilt and/or go up and down while in the air; to tilt, tilt the pilot wheel left/right; to go up and down, push the pilot wheel to go down, pull the pilot wheel to go up

How it will switch control?: When on ground: Steering Wheel   When in air: Pilot Wheel (basically automatic)
