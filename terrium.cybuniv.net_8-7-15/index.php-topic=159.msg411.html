Summer Development Plan
« on: July 01, 2015, 05:31:14 PM »
Dearest Terrians,


First of all, I wish to thank you for taking the time to check on Terrium's status and keep this forum alive. I am happy to report that, as of today, Terrium's summer development has officially started.

Unfortunately, our lead programmer, Arthur, has been on an indefinite leave of absence due to personal issues, and he may not return as a developer. Despite this loss, Terrium remains in competent and hard-working hands. I have personally hired a freelance developer to help us with the demanding work required to complete our goal for this summer—to release Terrium Beta 1.0. A friend of mine, Roe, will also be available for freelance web development when it comes time to improving the site and other back-end projects.

As we progress throughout the rewrite, I will try to keep you all better posted on milestones during development. I cannot wait to play this game with you all!


Sincerely,


Eric
