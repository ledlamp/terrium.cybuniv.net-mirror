I was playing in Seb's sky king and I drove around the curve to the ceiling, and I found out that I could drive on the ceiling for a short while until I lost contact and fell. How cool is that?!     


So all you need to do is drive on the curve until you are on the ceiling, and you will be driving on the ceiling for a moment until your buggy bounces or something and your tires loose contact, and as soon as your tires loose contact you fall off.



When I was about to record the video, my buggy rolled under the table and I discovered that the camera view warps when the object is very close.
