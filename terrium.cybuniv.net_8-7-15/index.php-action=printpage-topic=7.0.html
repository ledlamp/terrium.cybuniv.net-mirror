Jet handling: Yep, it's harder, and it's meant to be that way. There are a lot of maps where the Jet is just a pain to take off with, some where it's impossible. This is intentional.
"more handling"? I assume you mean you want us to increase the agility of the jet... We could do that but it would likely end up difficult to fly; the inertia isn't going to go anywhere, so being able to pitch back faster will simply lead to a stall.

Hovercraft: sounds like you were in vertical-flight mode in the hovercraft. Toggle between vertical flight and horizontal flight with spacebar. also, Q and E make you go up. And X or C is your brake (iirc).

Server settings: probably will stay hidden. They're actuallt still there just inaccessible to players. However, they will be included in map files - so map creators can mess with physics all they want, but at the same time actually design worlds to fit the physics, too.

Super-tanks, inverted gravity and unstable buggies were amusing, but Terrium isn't intended as a physics sandbox game. So, "Server Settings" are (hopefully) gone for good.
